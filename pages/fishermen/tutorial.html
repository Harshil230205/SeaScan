How to Use the App

1

Scan Your Fish

Tap the Scan button in the bottom navigation. Position your camera so the fish is clearly visible in the frame and tap to capture.

2

View Results

After scanning, you'll see the fish species, estimated weight, freshness score, and quality indicators. You can save these results to your catch history.

3

Track Your Catch

Visit the Dashboard to see your daily catch summary, history, and statistics. This helps you keep track of your fishing activities over time.

4

Access Hotspot Maps

Check the Hotspot Maps feature to find the best fishing locations based on recent catch data and seasonal patterns.
