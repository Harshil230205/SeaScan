About the System

AquaTrack is a comprehensive fishery management system designed to help fishermen track their catches, identify fish species, and access valuable data insights. The platform combines modern technology with traditional fishing knowledge to create a sustainable fishing ecosystem.

Our mission is to empower fishing communities with digital tools that enhance their livelihoods while promoting sustainable fishing practices and conservation of marine resources.

Key Features

  • Automated fish species identification using computer vision
  • Catch logging and tracking for individual fishermen
  • Historical data analysis and trends
  • Fishing hotspot mapping and recommendations
  • Community knowledge sharing and best practices
  • Compliance with fishing regulations and quotas
